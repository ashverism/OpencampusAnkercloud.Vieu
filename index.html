Upload LinkedIn Connections to Vieu

Download and upload the LinkedIn connections of DeliverHealth's leadership team and key introducers. This significantly expands Vieu's ability to find warm paths into target accounts.

1

Go to LinkedIn Settings

Log in to LinkedIn. Click your profile picture in the top-right corner, then select Settings & Privacy.

2

Navigate to Data Privacy

In the left sidebar, click Data Privacy. Then click Get a copy of your data.

3

Select Connections Data

Choose Want something in particular? Select the data files you're most interested in. Check the box for Connections only. Click Request archive.

LinkedIn will email you a download link within 10 minutes for connections data (vs. 24 hours for the full archive). Use this faster method.
4

Download the CSV

Click the link in the LinkedIn email. Download the file — it will be a .zip containing Connections.csv. Extract the CSV file.

5

Upload to Vieu

Log in to Vieu. Navigate to Network and select Upload LinkedIn CSV. Upload the Connections.csv file. Vieu will process and map the connections against your target accounts.

Repeat this for Sy, Andrea, and Kyle Hickok — each person's connections expands the warm path surface area for your 300 accounts.
Who to prioritize

Integrate Salesforce with Vieu

Connecting Salesforce allows Vieu to sync your existing pipeline, match Vieu intelligence against live opportunities, and support CRM attribution for the outcome-based reward reporting.

1

Access Vieu Integrations

Log in to Vieu. Navigate to Settings then Integrations. Select Salesforce.

2

Authenticate with Salesforce

Click Connect Salesforce. You will be redirected to Salesforce's OAuth login. Enter your Salesforce admin credentials and approve the Vieu integration.

You need Salesforce Admin or API-enabled user credentials to complete this step.
3

Map Your Objects

Select which Salesforce objects to sync: Accounts, Contacts, Opportunities. Map Vieu's enrichment fields to your existing Salesforce fields or create new custom fields.

4

Configure Sync Direction

Choose bidirectional sync or Vieu-to-Salesforce only. For DeliverHealth, Vieu recommends Vieu-to-Salesforce for enrichment fields and Salesforce-to-Vieu for account and opportunity status.

5

Set Up Attribution Tracking

Create a custom field on the Opportunity object: Vieu_Influenced__c (Checkbox) and Vieu_Meeting_Date__c (Date). These fields enable the closed-loop reporting required for the outcome-based fee structure.

This step is required for Vieu-influenced deal reward tracking per the MLA. Confirm with your Sales Ops hire on setup.
6

Run Initial Sync

Trigger the first full sync. Vieu will match your Salesforce accounts against the graph and begin enriching. Initial sync typically completes within 2-4 hours depending on account volume.

Priority: Low — complete after network setup

Integrate HubSpot with Vieu

If DeliverHealth uses HubSpot alongside or instead of Salesforce, connect it to Vieu for the same enrichment, sync, and attribution capabilities.

1

Access Vieu Integrations

Log in to Vieu. Navigate to Settings then Integrations. Select HubSpot.

2

Authenticate with HubSpot

Click Connect HubSpot. You will be redirected to HubSpot's OAuth flow. Log in with a HubSpot Super Admin account and grant the requested permissions.

Vieu requires access to Contacts, Companies, and Deals objects. Review the permissions screen and confirm before approving.
3

Select Your HubSpot Portal

If your HubSpot account has multiple portals, select the correct one for DeliverHealth's sales pipeline. Confirm the portal ID matches your active CRM.

4

Map Properties

Map Vieu enrichment data to HubSpot properties: Company enrichment to Company object, contact intelligence to Contact object, opportunity signals to Deal object.

5

Create Attribution Properties

In HubSpot, create two custom Deal properties: Vieu Influenced (Checkbox) and Vieu Meeting Date (Date picker). These support closed-loop reporting for outcome-based fees.

Same requirement as the Salesforce integration — needed for quarterly outcome reports per the MLA attribution section.
6

Configure Lists for Campaign Export

Set up a HubSpot list to receive Vieu-enriched NB contacts. When Vieu delivers each cohort CSV, contacts can be imported directly into this list for enrollment in HubSpot sequences or Outreach.io campaigns.
